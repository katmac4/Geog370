In this project I learned how to display images in different projections

Describe in your own words how you displayed the map in different projections using QGIS

The maps below have different projections of the world map. These were created by changing the ESRI and EPSG in QGIS.

WGS84 Projection

The continents on this map become wider at the most positive and most negative latitudes.

Aitoff Projection

This projection allows for the perception of the world as a sphere, and distortion is greater the further away from the equator a point is.

Mercator Projection

The shape of the continents is preserved but the area increases as you move away from the equator.

Sphere Winkel

This projection is similar to the aitoff projection, except there is more vertical stretch.

Conical Equidistant

Distortion of shape increases as you move away from the North Pole.

Cylindrical Equal Area

There is increasing shortening as you move away from the equator on both sides.

North Pole Azimuthal Equidistant

Similar to the conical equidistant projection however the widening at the southern latitudes is increased.

Sphere Mollweide

This projection has less vertical distortion than the Winkel projection and less curvature than the aitoff projection.

South Pole Orthographic

The entire world cannot be viewed in this projection and it is only useful for viewing Antarctica.
